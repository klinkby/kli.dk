Some months ago Yahoo! released it's Pipes site. It's propably the coolest example of web technology today. It allows
you to remix data from a wide range of sources like RSS feeds, web services, search machines, flickr and Google Base (
yes Yahoo! links to Google! content). Here's a simple pipe I made that
lists [ tonight's movies on the public TV stations in Denmark](http://pipes.yahoo.com/pipes/pipe.info?_id=sF5Z1bV03BGouwHyl7okhQ).
